Transformations Delivered: Success Stories

World Real Estate: Global Market Dominance

Challenge: The company had Russian teams, but an international department was non-existent (only 2 people, 1 deal from an agency). Despite it being a new industry for me, I took on the challenge. Key issues included reliance on low-commission agencies, lack of data-driven decision-making, and unmotivated teams with long deal cycles (1 deal per 3 months via agencies).

Strativise Action: Implemented reporting dashboards for data-based decisions. Shifted strategy from agencies to direct developer partnerships for higher commissions. Restructured and motivated the international team, optimizing processes.

Result: Within 9 months, expanded into 9 different regions. Achieved 49.8% profitability. Revenue grew 10x. MRR boosted from 0.6% to 10%. High-performing managers closed ~3 deals per month directly from developers, a significant uplift from the previous 1 deal per 3 months from agencies.
